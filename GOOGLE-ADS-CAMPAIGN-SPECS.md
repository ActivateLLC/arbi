## CAMPAIGN CREATION STEPS (Google Ads)

1. **Log into ads.google.com**
2. **Click "New Campaign"**
3. **Select goal:** Sales
4. **Choose type:** Display (easiest) or Video (if you have product videos)
5. **Enter campaign name** from above
6. **Set daily budget** from specs above
7. **Select locations:** United States
8. **Add audiences** from targeting section above
9. **Upload images** (all 3 sizes for each creative)
10. **Enter headlines and descriptions** (copy from above)
11. **Add final URL** from specs
12. **Review and publish**
13. **Campaign status:** "Eligible" or "Under review" (takes 1-2 business days)
