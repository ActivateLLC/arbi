## 📊 Recommended Workflow

### Phase 1: Free Discovery (Day 1)
1. **Meta Ad Library** - Download 10 ads per product
2. **BigSpy Free** - 3 searches to find top performers
3. **TikTok Creative Center** - Browse top ads
4. **Dropispy** - Search for e-commerce angle

**Output:** 30+ proven ad examples

### Phase 2: Analysis (Day 2)
1. Organize ads by product
2. Identify common patterns
3. Extract winning copy
4. Note top-performing formats

### Phase 3: Adaptation (Day 3)
1. Use Canva to recreate layouts
2. Replace copy with your offers
3. Update pricing
4. Export final assets

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. Look for Ads with High Engagement
- Lots of comments = people are interested
- Lots of shares = compelling value prop
- Running for 6+ months = proven winner

### 2. Check Multiple Platforms
- Same product, different ad formats
- Learn what works where
- Cross-platform inspiration

### 3. Study Top Spenders
- If they're spending millions, it's working
- Copy their structure, not exact creative
- Learn from their testing

### 4. Seasonal Patterns
- Look for "Black Friday" ads (urgency tactics)
- "Holiday gift" ads (gift-focused messaging)
- "New Year" ads (transformation messaging)

### 5. Landing Page Analysis
- Click through to see full funnel
- Screenshot landing pages
- Note offer structure and guarantees

## 🔒 Legal & Ethical Considerations

✅ **ALLOWED:**
- Viewing public ad libraries
- Downloading ads for research
- Using as inspiration
- Adapting layouts and concepts
- Learning from competitors

❌ **NOT ALLOWED:**
- Copying exact creative without changes
- Using competitor logos/trademarks
- Claiming competitor reviews
- Stealing proprietary product photos

**Rule of Thumb:** Transform, don't copy. Use as inspiration, create your own version.
