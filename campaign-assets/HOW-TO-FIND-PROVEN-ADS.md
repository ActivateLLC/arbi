### 2. TikTok Creative Center
**URL:** https://ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter

**How to Use:**
1. Go to "Top Ads" section
2. Filter by:
   - Country: United States
   - Industry: Home & Garden (for desk), Smart Home (for security), Home Appliances (for espresso)

**How to Use:**
1. Search for advertiser names (FlexiSpot, Ring, Breville)
2. View their active campaigns
3. See what creative formats they're using
4. Note their messaging and offers

## 📥 How to Download Assets

### From Meta Ad Library:
1. **For Images:**
   - Right-click on image
   - "Open image in new tab"
   - Right-click → "Save image as..."
   - Save to `/campaign-assets/[product-id]/`

2. **For Videos:**
   - Click the video to expand
   - Right-click on video
   - "Save video as..."
   - OR use browser inspector (F12) → Network tab → reload → find .mp4 file

3. **For Copy:**
   - Highlight ad text
   - Copy to text file
   - Save as `ad-copy-examples.txt`

### Browser Extension (Recommended):
- **Video DownloadHelper** (Firefox/Chrome)
- **FB Video Downloader** (Chrome)
- **Image Downloader** (Chrome)

## 🎨 What to Look For in Ads

### High-Performing Visual Elements:
✅ **For Standing Desks:**
- Person sitting vs standing (before/after)
- Desk going up and down (motion/GIF)
- Multiple monitor setup
- Wide desktop surface emphasized
- "Relieves back pain" messaging

✅ **For Security Systems:**
- Split-screen showing multiple camera views
- Night vision footage
- Smartphone app interface
- Motion detection alert demo
- "Peace of mind" emotional hook

✅ **For Espresso Machines:**
- Coffee being pulled (satisfying visuals)
- Milk frothing/latte art
- Side-by-side: coffee shop price vs home
- Calculator showing yearly savings
- "Barista quality at home" messaging

### High-Converting Copy Patterns:
1. **Problem-Agitate-Solution:**
   - "Sitting 8 hours a day destroying your back?"
   - "Tired of $6 lattes?"
   - "Worried about home security?"

2. **Savings-Focused:**
   - "Save $2,190/year by making coffee at home"
   - "Same quality as $2,000 desks for $599"
   - "Protect your $300k home with $899"

3. **Social Proof:**
   - "50,000+ 5-star reviews"
   - "Rated #1 by Wirecutter"
   - "As seen on Shark Tank"

4. **Urgency:**
   - "Limited time: Save $300"
   - "Sale ends tonight"
   - "Only 15 left in stock"

## 🔄 How to Adapt Downloaded Ads

### Step 1: Organize Downloads
```
campaign-assets/
├── standing-desk-pro/
│   ├── competitor-ads/
│   │   ├── flexispot-ad-1.jpg
│   │   ├── autonomous-video-1.mp4
│   │   └── uplift-copy.txt
│   └── adapted/
│       ├── our-ad-1.jpg (edited version)
│       └── our-video-1.mp4
```

### Step 2: Edit Assets
**For Images:**
- Use Canva or Photoshop
- Replace competitor logo with "Arbi" or remove
- Change price to your price ($599, $899, $799)
- Update product name
- Keep the layout, colors, style

**For Videos:**
- Use CapCut or Adobe Premiere
- Replace text overlays with your copy
- Change final CTA to your URL
- Keep the pacing and music style
- Maintain the hook structure

**For Copy:**
- Change product specs to match yours
- Update pricing
- Keep the emotional hooks and structure
- Maintain urgency/scarcity elements

### Step 3: Create Variations
For each winning ad, create 3 variations:
- **A:** Different headline
- **B:** Different CTA
- **C:** Different image/video

## 🎯 Best Practices

### Legal Considerations:
✅ **Allowed:**
- Using ads as inspiration
- Copying layouts and structures
- Adapting messaging strategies
- Learning from competitors

❌ **Not Allowed:**
- Copying exact images without modification
- Using competitor logos
- Claiming competitor awards/reviews
- Exact word-for-word copy

### Quality Standards:
- Minimum resolution: 1200x1200 for images
- Minimum video quality: 1080p
- File size: Under 5MB per asset
- Format: JPG for images, MP4 for videos
