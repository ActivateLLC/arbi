## Quick Video Creation (30 mins each)

### Using Canva (Easiest):

1. **Go to Canva.com/create/videos**
2. **Search "Product Ad Video"** templates
3. **Choose 15-20 second template**
4. **Customize:**
   - Replace product images with standing desk/security camera/espresso machine
   - Edit text overlays with script from above
   - Add your logo
   - Choose background music from Canva library
5. **Export as MP4** (1080p)
6. **Upload to YouTube** as Unlisted
7. **Copy URL** for Google Ads

### Using Stock Footage + CapCut:

1. **Download free stock videos** from:
   - Pexels.com/videos
   - Pixabay.com/videos
   - Unsplash.com/videos

2. **Search for:**
   - "office desk", "standing desk", "home office"
   - "security camera", "home security", "night vision"
   - "espresso", "coffee making", "barista"

3. **Edit in CapCut:**
   - Import clips (15 seconds total)
   - Add text overlays with script
   - Add transitions (fade, zoom)
   - Add background music
   - Export as 1080p MP4

4. **Upload to YouTube**

## Option 3: Simple Slideshow Videos (15 mins) 📸

**If you don't want to create full videos:**

1. **Gather 5-7 product images**
2. **Use Canva Video Slideshow template**
3. **Each image displays 2-3 seconds**
4. **Add animated text overlays**:
   - Slide 1: Product name
   - Slide 2: Key feature
   - Slide 3: Price + CTA
5. **Add music**
6. **Export**

This still counts as a "video" for Google Ads!

## Video Ad Targeting Settings

Once videos are created and uploaded to YouTube:

### Campaign Setup:
1. **Campaign type:** Video
2. **Campaign subtype:** Drive conversions
3. **Budget:** $30-40/day per campaign
4. **Bidding:** Maximum CPV (cost per view) = $0.10

### Video Ad Format:
- **Skippable in-stream ads** (most common)
- **Non-skippable ads** (15 sec max, more expensive)
- **Bumper ads** (6 seconds, quick brand awareness)

**Recommended: Skippable in-stream** (15-20 sec videos)

### Targeting (Same as Display campaigns):
- Locations: United States
- Age: 25-54
- Interests: In-market audiences (from previous specs)
- Placements: YouTube search results, YouTube videos
- Topics: Home improvement, Technology, Food & dining

## YouTube Video Requirements (Google Ads)

✅ **Accepted formats:** MP4, MOV, AVI, MPEG, WMV, FLV, 3GPP
✅ **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (1080p) recommended
✅ **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (landscape), 1:1 (square), 9:16 (vertical)
✅ **Maximum file size:** 1GB
✅ **Maximum length:** 3 minutes (but 15-30 sec performs best)
✅ **Audio:** Required (music or voice-over)

## Video Performance Tips

### Hook viewers in first 3 seconds:
- ❌ "Welcome to our channel..."
- ✅ "Sitting all day is killing your back"
- ✅ "Spending $5/day on coffee?"
- ✅ "Is your home protected at night?"

### Strong CTA at end:
- "Shop Now"
- "Learn More"
- "Get Yours Today"
- "Limited Time Offer"

### Test multiple versions:
- Version A: Focus on problem ("Back pain from sitting")
- Version B: Focus on solution ("Standing desk benefits")
- Version C: Focus on price ("Save $1,000/year")

**Google Ads will auto-optimize** to show best-performing version.
